Twentieth-Century Drama will contain 2,100 plays in English from around the world from the 1890s to 2003. Unlike other drama collections available today, it presents a global, inclusive collection of the most important plays written in the English language from all over the world, highlighting many nationalities and ethnicities, and offering the most and useful complete picture of twentieth century drama.  It contains mostly copyright texts unavailable elsewhere in electronic form including many out-of-print works that are difficult to obtain.
